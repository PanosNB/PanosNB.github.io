About Us

We are immigrants to New Zealand, bringing with us the rich tradition of modal music from the Near East. Tropoi offers authentic auditory experiences, allowing you to explore this vibrant and often under-represented musical heritage.

Our music celebrates the intricate melodic themes found in makams, dastgāhs, and dromoi, akin to Western scales. The name "Tropoi" is inspired by the musical scales of Byzantine chant, celebrated for their harmonious and resonant qualities.

Our Music

At the heart of Tropoi's immersive experience is the ancient practice of pure intonation, a system where musical pitches are derived from natural harmonic frequencies. This method creates melodies that resonate deeply within the human body, fostering a profound sense of relaxation and connection.

By blending pure, enharmonic sounds with unique rhythms and traditional instruments, Tropoi offers a genuine sonic journey that transports you to the Near East.

New Album: Nostalgia

Album Art

We're excited to announce that our album, "Nostalgia," is now available on all major platforms!

Nostalgia comprises 10 original compositions using traditional instruments, rhythms and scales (i.e., tropoi, dastgāhs or makams) of the Near East.

Instruments: Oud, Santur, Kemanche (Lyra), Qajak, Saz, Setar, Tar, Violin, Guitar and traditional percussion.

Traditional Rhythms: Shish-o-Hasht (6/8), Kalamatiano (7/8), Tsifteteli (4/4), Chania Syrtos (8/4), 2-Step Syrtos (4/4), Zembekiko (9/4), Karsilamas (9/8), Tsamiko (3/4), Bayon (4/4)
