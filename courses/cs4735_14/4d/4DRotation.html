3D and 4D Models and Rotations


Press the appropriate keys to view the models and rotations, listed below:

Select a model:
3D Cube: "3" 4D Cube: "4"
 
Select a rotation:
 
3D Rotation Axis:
Press "x" for x axis,"y" for y axis,"z" for z axis
 
4D Rotation Plane:
Press "a" for xy plane, "s" for xz plane,"d" for xw plane,
Press "f" for yz plane, "g" for yw plane, "h" for zw plane
